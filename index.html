Features

Everything a school office needs, in one place.

Replace paper registers, WhatsApp groups and scattered Excel sheets with a single system for admissions, fees, attendance and results.

Student Registration

Digital admission forms with document uploads, B-form verification and instant roll-number assignment.

Fee Management

Generate monthly challans, accept cash or bank transfers, and track pending dues per class automatically.

Attendance Tracking

Mark attendance from a teacher's phone in seconds. Parents are notified on the same day, every time.

WhatsApp Alerts

Fee reminders, attendance updates and exam schedules — delivered where parents actually read them.

Exam Results

Upload marks once, generate gradebooks, printable report cards and percentile rankings automatically.

Admin Dashboard
